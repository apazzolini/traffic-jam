Traffic Jam

On the 2012 Credera company trip, we had a competition with teams of 8 to solve this while moving in real life. None of the groups were able to solve it in the limited time we had, but it was a fun puzzle. I wanted to figure out a generic algorithm, and spent the plane ride back making this game and deriving it. Blog

Rules of the game: Swap the players facing right with the players facing left, such that each group ends up on the opposite end of the line. You may move forward into an empty spot, and you may jump over a person facing you, provided the spot behind them is empty.
