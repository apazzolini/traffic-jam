Traffic Jam

On the 2012 Credera company trip, we had a competition with teams of 8 to solve this while moving in real life. None of the groups were able to solve it in the limited time we had, but it was a fun puzzle. I wanted to figure out a generic algorithm, and spent the plane ride back making this game and deriving it.
